Shirley Temple

Can you ever go wrong with a shirley temple? If you said anything other than no, we're no longer friends

Here's how you can make the perfect shirley temple

Ingredients

  • Ice
  • 1 (12 ounce) bottle Grenadine
  • 1 (2 Liter) bottle lemon-lime soda
  • Maraschino cherries for garnish, optional

Instructions

  1. Fill a glass with Ice
  2. Add 1 ounce (2 tbsp) grenadine
  3. top with 8 ounces lemon-lime soda
  4. Garnish with maraschino cherries if desired

Notes

Soda: Modern Shirley Temples are made with white soda such as Sprite or 7UP. For a more traditional approach, substitute ginger ale.

I Personally add a splash of orange juice to my shirley temple, but it's up to preference (you should try it though).
